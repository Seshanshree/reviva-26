ABOUT REVIVA'26

UNLEASHING INNOVATION, INSPIRING BRILLIANCE

🐦‍🔥

WHAT IS REVIVA'26?

REVIVA'26 is a prestigious National Level Technical Symposium organized by the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, Government College of Engineering – Bargur (Autonomous). It is a platform where brilliant minds from across the nation gather to showcase their technical prowess, creativity, and innovative thinking. With a thunderous lineup of events spanning hardware, software, design, and problem-solving domains, REVIVA'26 promises an electrifying experience for every participant.

🏛️

ABOUT THE INSTITUTION

Government College of Engineering – Bargur (GCE Bargur) is an autonomous institution affiliated with Anna University, Tamil Nadu. Renowned for academic excellence, cutting-edge research facilities, and a culture of innovation that has produced some of the brightest engineering minds in the country.

📡

ECE DEPARTMENT

The Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering stands at the forefront of technological advancement. With state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculty, and an unwavering commitment to excellence, the ECE department continues to push the boundaries of what's possible in electronics and communications.

OUR VISION

We envision our students to be excellent engineers not only in the field of science and technology, but also embed the greatest values of human life. Our commitment lies in producing good citizens, comprehensive knowledge seekers and remains as an asset in building a strong and developed nation.

🌟

WHY PARTICIPATE?

Compete with the best minds nationally, win exciting cash prizes, earn certificates of excellence, network with industry professionals and peers, gain hands-on technical exposure, and be part of an unforgettable two-day celebration of technology and creativity.
